Danyang–Kunshan Grand Bridge

History

The Danyang–Kunshan Grand Bridge is located in China and is officially the longest bridge in the world. The bridge stretches about 164.8 kilometers and forms part of the Beijing–Shanghai High-Speed Railway. Construction began in 2006 and was completed in 2010 before opening to the public in 2011. The bridge crosses rivers, lakes, canals, and farmland. Thousands of workers and engineers participated in its construction. Today it is one of the greatest achievements of modern civil engineering.
